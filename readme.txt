Embed surveys from SurveyHero into your WordPress website.

== Description ==

This is the official WordPress plugin for SurveyHero. Using this plugin, you will be able to embed surveys you have created in your SurveyHero account, directly into your WordPress website or blog.

SurveyHero makes it easy for you to create beautiful and mobile optimized online surveys for free! You can create surveys with unlimited questions and get unlimited responses.

If you have not yet signed up for your free SurveyHero account, you can do so here: [Sign up](https://www.surveyhero.com/signup)

Please feel free to contact us any time if you have any questions: [Contact Support](https://www.surveyhero.com/contact)

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Installation Instructions =

Below you will find a link to a help article which explains everything you need to know about getting started with the plugin.

See here: [How to Embed your Surveys into your WordPress Website](https://help.surveyhero.com/manual/collect-responses/how-to-embed-your-surveys-into-your-wordpress-website)

= Do I need a SurveyHero account to use this plugin? =

Yes, you can sign up for free here: [Sign up](https://www.surveyhero.com/signup)

= What do I do after I have installed the plugin? =

When you have the plugin installed, you will need to go to your survey in your SurveyHero account and copy the survey's embed code.

You can find out more here: [How to Embed your Surveys into your WordPress Website](https://help.surveyhero.com/manual/collect-responses/how-to-embed-your-surveys-into-your-wordpress-website)

= How do I change the embedded survey settings? =

You can manage the settings for your embedded survey from the Embed Collector settings in your SurveyHero account. This will allow you to change the size and participation settings for your survey without having to update the embed code.

You can find out more here: [How to Embed Surveys into your Website](https://help.surveyhero.com/manual/collect-responses/how-to-embed-surveys-into-your-website)

= I have some questions about my survey, how can I contact support? =

If you have any questions about the plugin or your survey, please feel free to contact our support team here: [Contact Support](https://www.surveyhero.com/contact)
